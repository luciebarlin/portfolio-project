Hi!

I'm Lucie.
Pastry chef turned frontend developer

In 2022 I made the difficult decision to leave my career of almost a decade as a pastry chef, and begin retraining as a developer.

In the last 8 months I’ve come from almost no knowledge of web development to being confident using HTML, CSS, Sass, and JavaScript.

Working in the high-pressure environment of the kitchen means I’m no stranger to working hard, consistently, and to specification. I also like to be organised and I have high standards for myself.

I am currently focusing on learning the more advanced parts of web accessibility. Disabled users account for at least 16% of the market, and making sure we can reach them (without compromising usability) is an exciting challenge for me.
